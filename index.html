Golden State Warriors Fun Facts

Fact 1: The Golden State Warriors were founded in 1946 and originally based in Philadelphia.
Fact 2: They moved to San Francisco in 1962 and later to Oakland in 1971, becoming the Golden State Warriors.
Fact 3: The Warriors have won multiple NBA championships, including recent wins in 2015, 2017, 2018, and 2022.
Fact 4: Stephen Curry, widely regarded as one of the best shooters in NBA history, has played his entire career with the Warriors.
Fact 5: The Warriors' mascot, "Thunder," was used until 2008, after which it was retired when the team rebranded.
Fact 6: The team moved to the Chase Center in San Francisco in 2019, which became their current home stadium.
